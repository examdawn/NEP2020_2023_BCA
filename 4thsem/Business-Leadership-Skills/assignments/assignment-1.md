## Q1. Meaning/definition of leadership.

Leadership is when a person leads another, or a group effectively. 
- This is different from being a boss
    - boss's role has no scope beyond ensuring the work gets done. 

A leader ensures that the team is effectively pulling it off while promoting healthy interaction and personal/professional development
- This is a very social role
- In practice, it has little ties to bosses and management

## Q2. What do you mean by a modern leader?
A modern leader is all about connecting employees to purpose, accomplishment and one another. 
- They show how their employees’ work makes a difference, how it furthers the company’s purpose and why it matters. 
- Modern leaders give their employees the skills and encouragement to achieve great things
- This is done all while bringing people together.

## Q3. Explain the characteristics of leadership.

Leadership is not a role, but an ability we use to lead people and help them grow. Good Leaders show the following traits:
- Influence
    - A good leader is able to positively influence the team
    - This also includes motivating them beyond monetary compensation and connecting them to project vision
- Active Innovation
    - A good leader encourages innovation and optimization
    - This also means that the leader is ready to take risks and listen to his team/peers
- Integrity
    - A good leader takes responsibility for their decisions and the actions of their team
    - This also includes being honest and making decisions in good standing
- Decisiveness
    - This includes taking rapid and accurate decisions in any situation
    - This ensures that the leader is able to effectively lead even in tough, time constrained situations
- Resilience
    - A leader always fails and grows from their mistakes
